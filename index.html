About Us

Advanced Educational Creativity Management (AECM) specializes in providing scientific and educational consultations, managing universities, institutes, and schools, and developing modern educational programs and technologies. We aim to lead educational innovation and build institutions that meet both local and international standards.

Our Services

  • Educational consulting for schools, universities, and institutions
  • Management of private and international schools
  • Development of bilingual and STEAM-based curricula
  • Training programs for teachers and administrators
  • Technology integration in classrooms
  • School licensing and quality assurance systems
